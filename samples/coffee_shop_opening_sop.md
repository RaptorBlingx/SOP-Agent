# Coffee Shop Opening SOP

## Purpose

Prepare the coffee station safely and consistently before opening to customers.

## Preconditions

- The store manager or assigned opener is on site.
- Power and water supply are available.
- Cleaning supplies and coffee ingredients are stocked.

## Procedure

1. Unlock the front door and disable the alarm.
2. Turn on the lights in the seating area, counter area, and kitchen.
3. Wash hands and put on food-safe gloves if handling ingredients.
4. Check that the espresso machine water line is connected and the drip tray is empty.
5. Turn on the espresso machine and allow it to warm up for at least 10 minutes.
6. Fill the grinder hopper with coffee beans if the level is below one-third full.
7. Brew one test espresso shot and discard it.
8. Refill milk, syrups, cups, lids, napkins, and stirrers at the service counter.
9. Start a fresh batch of drip coffee and label the brew time.
10. Wipe the counter, register screen, and handoff area with approved sanitizer.
11. Confirm the cash drawer float is present and matches the opening balance sheet.
12. Unlock the front door for customers at the scheduled opening time.

## Safety Checks

- Do not operate the espresso machine if there is a visible leak.
- Do not serve drip coffee older than 30 minutes.
- Report any missing cash or equipment malfunction to the manager immediately.

## Escalation

If equipment fails during opening, contact the store manager and pause beverage service until the issue is resolved.
